CALIN RENT CAR is a simple catalog app. Users can view available cars and contact us by phone or email. The app does not ask for accounts, does not store personal details, and does not share any user data.

We do not collect, store, process, sell, or share personal or sensitive user information. The app is limited to showing car details and opening your phone or email app.

The app does not request access to location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage, or device identifiers.

When you tap call or email, the app opens your phone dialer or your email client. Messages are sent by your own app.
